Project Story 🌷

The cosmetic industry changes rapidly with trends influenced by social media, customer reviews, product innovation, and beauty culture.

Using Tableau dashboards, this project helps visualize customer behavior, top-selling products, cosmetic categories, and emerging beauty trends.

Our goal is transforming beauty industry data into actionable insight.
